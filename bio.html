Sebastian Sonne

Hey there!
I'm Sebastian, originally from the charming south of Germany, with a detour through the vibrant streets of Singapore for 3 1/2 years. Now, I'm Texas-bound as an exchange student, soaking up the Texan experience.

Beyond the classroom, I'm weaving my own tale of education and adventure. From the cobblestone streets of home to the skyscrapers of Singapore, each place has etched its mark on my journey.

Now, let's dive into my digital playground. I'm not just a student; I'm a coding enthusiast crafting innovative projects and turning ideas into tech realities. Join me as we unravel the digital tapestry!

When I'm not capturing moments through my lens, you'll find me grounded, exploring the beauty of the great outdoors. My love for photography meets a passion for travel, although more occasional than constant. Whether it's hiking in the mountains or seeking hidden gems, each shot tells a story.

And of course, there's tennis. Join me on this dynamic court, where strategy, skill, and passion converge.

Embark on this journey with me, where each click, code, and court serve tells a unique story!
